What does RGB stand for?

Red, Green, Blue
Red, Gray, Black
Royal, Green, Blue
Red, Gold, Brown

Correct! ✓

RGB stands for Red, Green, Blue - the three primary colors of light used in digital displays.

Detailed Explanation: The RGB color model is an additive color model where red, green, and blue light are combined in various ways to reproduce a broad array of colors. This model is used in electronic displays such as computer monitors, television screens, and smartphone displays. When all three colors are combined at full intensity, they create white light.
