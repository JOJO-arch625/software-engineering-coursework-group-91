## Section Notes

### Header

- Keep consistent with the full team site.
- Include quick access to homepage and navigation.
- Put language, font-size, and sound controls here.

### Hero

- Introduce the purpose of the game page clearly.
- Explain that users learn by playing.

### Score Strip

- Adds game feeling and instant progress visibility.
- Should show points, streak, and current status.

### Model Match

- Uses real design or media scenarios.
- Helps users decide when to use RGB, CMYK, HSV, or YCbCr.
- Gives instant feedback and explanation.

### Hue Lab

- Lets users adjust hue, saturation, and lightness directly.
- Helps users understand colour properties visually.
- Gives a match percentage and encouragement.

### Support Panels

- Shows safe interaction design.
- Connects this page to other pages in the coursework site.
